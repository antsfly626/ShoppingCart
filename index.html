Gardeing Tips

  1. Know your zone and what can grow there (Southern California is zone 10)
  2. Don't underwater or overwater your plants. Rotting leaves is a sign of overwatering and dry, brown leaves is one of underwatering.
  3. Keep houseplants by sunny windows.
  4. You can multiply some plants by propogating cuttings and rooting them in water. You can also collect seeds from some plants.
